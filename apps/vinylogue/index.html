an iOS APP for LAST.FM

"What albums were my friends and I listening to last year?"

  • View your weekly album charts from exactly one year ago.
  • Slide right to go further back in time.
  • Add or import friends.
  • Check out their past charts.
  • Reminisce!
  • Dig into the details of your albums.
  • See your all-time play count and read about the album.

What do I need to get started?

  1. Your active last.fm username.
  2. An iPhone or iPod Touch with iOS 15+.
  3. A free copy of vinylogue!
